Felicity Smoak

Background

She is a hacker and the third member of the Arrow team. Her code name is Overwatch, when the team is on missions. She is also the "advisor" of the green arrow to the outside world.

Early Life

She had attended MIT for a degree in Cyber Security and Computer Science. She was also part of goth hacker group with her former boyfriend, in which they created a virus to expose government secrets.

Current Life

Felicity still works with Oliver Queen in his quest as the green arrow. She ussually tracks down the enemies, and controls the intercoms between the team. She also works with a hacker group known as Helix. She has been in and out of the team, but she is always on Oliver's side.
