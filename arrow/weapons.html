Weapons

Many weapons are used in the TV show, but here are the main ones.

Oliver's First Bow

This bow was given by Yao fei, his first teacher, this bow was used for the first season. It is a Carbon Fiber Long bow.

Curtis' T-Spheres

Due to the lack of fighting skills, Curtis created two T-Spheres to fight for him. One T-sphere is made for offensive combat while the other is used for defensive purposes.

Stun Guns

These Stun Guns are non-leathal and are used by both John Diggle and Wild Dog. They are made to look like real guns.
