Missions

Oliver Queen has been on missions constantly for 10 years. Here are some of the missions he has been on.

Alpha and Omega Virus Outbreak

This mission took place in Hong Kong, when Oliver was on the "island" Amanda Waller recruited Oliver and Maseo Yamashiro to find and erase the Alpha and Omega Virus. These two drugs together would have destroyed China.

Ra's Al Ghul

Ra's Al Ghul, in arabic means the Demon's Head. He was the leader of the League of Assasins. Oliver had to work under cover as Al Sa Him, to take down Ra's. He worked along side Malcom and his team, to take out the leader. After Ra's died, Malcom became the new Ra's Al Ghul.

Prometheus

Adrian Chase is the villian know as Prometheus. He worked alongside Oliver, as his lawyer. Adrian had the same training as Oliver, and he was his greatest enemy. He had kept Oliver captive for a week, until he told him his biggest secret. For his Final Act, he made Oliver go to Lian Yu with his whole team, then he blew up the whole island.
