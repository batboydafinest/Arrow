Oliver Queen

Background

Oliver Queen is the Green Arrow, and he founded this team. He works as the mayor of Star City, and at night he fights crime as the green arrow. He has training from the League Of Assasins, Deathstroke, and Thea Al Ghul. He is also part of the Bratva, the Russian Mafia. He is also a billionare and formally owned Verdant, a night club, and Queen Consolidated, a family company.

Early Life

Oliver befriend Tommy Merlyn, a family friend, and Laurel Lance. The Three of them were great friends, until Oliver Queen went on a cruise, named the Queen's Gambit, which broke down, causing him to be stranded on Lian Yu. There he met a man named Yao Fei, who was Oliver's first teacher. He tought Oliver how to use a bow and arrow.

After the Island

After he came back from the Island, he started his secret project, where he baecame the hood. He fought crime and took out villians, from his dad's list of powerful people.
