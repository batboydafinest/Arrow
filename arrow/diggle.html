John Diggle

Background

John Diggle is the second person to join team arrow. He uses his stun guns to fight crime, and his code name is Spartan. He is a very close friend to Oliver Queen, and was his former body-guard.

Early life

Before joining team arrow, John used to work for the millatary in Afganistan with his brother, Andy. Soon after, he quit and became a bodyguard for Oliver Queen.

Current life

John Diggle is one of the oldest team members in team arrow, and he trains most of the new recruits. His wife, Lyla, is the head of Argus, which is a big agency to lock-up threats to the US.
